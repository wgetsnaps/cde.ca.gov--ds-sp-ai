School and District Reports

Learn more about a school or district using reports on California Assessment of Student Performance and Progress (CAASPP), Local Control Funding Formula (LCFF), Adequate Yearly Progress (AYP), Alternative Schools Accountability Model (ASAM), California English Language Development Test (CELDT), English learners, enrollment, expulsion, and staffing.

Enter a portion of a school or district name. Name suggestions will appear after three or more characters are entered. After selecting a school/district name from the suggestion list, a list of links to various reports will be displayed. If a suggestion list does not appear, use the Submit button to get a list of schools/districts.
