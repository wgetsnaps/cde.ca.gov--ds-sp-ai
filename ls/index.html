Learning Support

Provides resources and instructional strategies that give students the physical, social, emotional, and intellectual support needed to learn.

Highlights

Bullying and Hate Motivated Behavior Prevention
Provides resources for parents, administrators, and students on how bullying can be prevented and addressed. Resources include publications, sample policies, and frequently asked questions.

Clearinghouse for Multilingual Documents (CMD)
The CMD is a secure database to assist local educational agency (LEA) staff in locating documents translated into non-English languages.

Flu Prevention
Information for controlling the outbreak of the flu in schools, including information on pandemic influenza.

Pertussis (Whooping Cough) Vaccine Requirement
Information on AB 354, the pertussis (whooping cough) vaccine booster (Tdap) requirement, and resources for local educational agencies and parents/guardians.
